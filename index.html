Biography

I am Jason Zhang (张瑞轩), an undergraduate student at the University of Toronto's Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. I love to learn, and am passionate about tackling tough interdisciplinary engineering challenges spanning software and hardware engineering. My interests involve machine learning, computer architecture, control theory, image processing, and augmented reality.

I spent my summer as a research assistant with the Discovery program, where I studied pedagogical development in engineering graduate students using thematic and sentiment analysis. Our work also involved applied natural language processing using NLTK in Python.

Outside of school, I'm also a member of the electrical division of the University of Toronto Aerospace Team's Space Systems group and the mechatronics division of the University of Toronto Chemical Vehicles team, where I'm working on building circuitry and programming for next-generation satellites and chemically-powered/controlled vehicles.

For a sense of what I'm learning, I also publish my notes.

I'm currently seeking summer 2024 internships, and am interested in exciting interdisciplinary opportunities in software engineering, embedded systems, and digital hardware/electronics.
